# LeadFlow Workflow

## Overview

LeadFlow follows a structured automation pipeline to transform raw business website discoveries into clean, validated, and CRM-ready lead data.

## Workflow

1. **Keyword Discovery**

   * Search for businesses using target keywords.

2. **Website Collection**

   * Collect candidate business websites from search results.

3. **Validation**

   * Verify that each website is active and relevant.

4. **Data Cleaning**

   * Normalize and standardize collected information.

5. **Quality Assurance**

   * Remove duplicates and invalid records.

6. **CRM Export**

   * Generate structured output ready for CRM import.

## Benefits

* Reduces manual lead research
* Improves data quality
* Produces consistent, structured datasets
* Supports scalable lead generation workflows
